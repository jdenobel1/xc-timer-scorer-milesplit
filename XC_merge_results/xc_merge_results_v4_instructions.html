Instructions for Milesplit CSV to TXT Converter for Results:

  1. Go to the Milesplit Meet Manager and download your entries as a CSV.
  2. Click the first button, Browse Milesplit CSV File.
  3. Click the second button, Convert to Boys and Girls CSV | TXT files.
  4. Click the third button, Open CC_Scorer.
  5. With the CC_Scorer file open, open a boys or girls output text file and copy all of the contents.
  6. Go back to the CC_Scorer file and make sure you are on the Entrants tab, then paste the content you had copied.
  7. Fix the bibs as necessary.
  8. You will do the same for the other gender.
  9. Open XNoteStopWatch if necessary.
